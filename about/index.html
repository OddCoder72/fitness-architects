About

Omar Khalid is our main fitness instructor. He is a proud alumnus of both the University of British Columbia and the University of Western Ontario, where he studied nutritional sciences and kinesiology. With over 20 years of training experience, he has trained various award-winning dance and sports teams. Omar has also developed connections with many therapists, who have assisted him throughout his career.
